Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between UX and UI design?
UX (User Experience) design focuses on the overall feel and functionality of a product, ensuring it's intuitive and meets user needs. UI (User Interface) design deals with the visual elements—colors, typography, and layout—that users interact with.
How long does a typical UI/UX design project take?
Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website redesign might take 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive application design could take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines after our initial discovery phase.
Do you provide design assets for development?
Yes, we deliver all necessary design assets including style guides, component libraries, and developer handoff files to ensure smooth implementation of our designs.
Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?
Absolutely! We can work within your existing brand guidelines or help you establish new ones. Our goal is to create designs that align with and enhance your brand identity.
Do you conduct user testing as part of your process?
Yes, usability testing is a critical part of our design process. We conduct tests at various stages to validate design decisions and ensure the final product meets user expectations.
