Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom software development take?

The timeline varies based on project complexity, features and requirements. Simple applications may take 2-3 months, while enterprise solutions can take 6-12 months. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial discovery phase.

How much does custom software cost?

Costs depend on project scope, complexity, and technology stack. We offer transparent pricing models - fixed price for well-defined projects and time & materials for evolving requirements.

How do you ensure the software meets our needs?

We follow an iterative development approach with regular demos and feedback sessions. You'll see progress at every stage and have opportunities to request adjustments, ensuring the final product aligns perfectly with your expectations.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive support packages including bug fixes, updates, feature enhancements, performance monitoring, and security patches. Our support can be scaled to match your operational needs.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely! Our solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with your current software ecosystem. We have extensive experience with APIs, data migration, and creating middleware for smooth interoperability.
