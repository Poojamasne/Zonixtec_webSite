At Zonixtec IT Services Pvt. Ltd., we don't just deliver technology—we create possibilities. Based in Baner, Pune, we are a fast-growing IT company specializing in custom software development, digital transformation, IT consulting, application testing, and end-to-end IT services. Our mission is to empower startups, enterprises, and businesses worldwide with scalable and result-driven digital solutions.

Joining our team means being part of a collaborative culture where Innovation, creativity, and growth come together. We work on diverse projects across industries, giving our employees hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies and exposure to solving real-world business challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for a job or internship at Zonixtec?

Yes, You can explore our current openings on the Careers page and apply directly by submitting your resume. For internships, keep an eye out for seasonal opportunities we post on the same page. Stay Connected!

What is the interview process like at Zonixtec?

Our interview process is designed to be simple and transparent. It usually includes an initial screening, a technical/skills assessment, and a final round discussion with our team.

Do you offer internships for students and fresh graduates?

Yes! We welcome students and recent graduates for internship opportunities where they can learn, gain hands-on experience, and possibly transition into full-time roles.

What qualities do you look for in candidates?

We value curiosity, problem-solving ability, teamwork, and a willingness to learn. Strong fundamentals, passion for technology, and a growth mindset are always a plus.

How can I prepare for my interview at Zonixtec?

We recommend revisiting your basics, showcasing your projects, and being ready to talk about real-life problem-solving experiences. Above all, be yourself—we value authenticity!
